Today's Menu

espresso

A bold and intense shot of pure coffee bliss! Perfect for those who love a strong caffeine kick with a rich, velvety crema. Best enjoyed on its own or as the base for your favorite coffee drinks.

Cappuccino

A classic Italian delight with the perfect balance of espresso, creamy steamed milk, and a fluffy milk foam topping. Light, airy, and irresistibly smooth—ideal for a cozy coffee break!

Latte

Smooth, creamy, and oh-so-satisfying! Our latte combines freshly brewed espresso with silky steamed milk, creating a rich and mellow coffee experience. Customize it with a shot of vanilla or caramel!

Americano

A simple yet flavorful coffee made by adding hot water to rich espresso. Smooth, mild, and full-bodied—perfect for those who enjoy a strong but less intense coffee flavor.

Mocha

The perfect blend of chocolate and coffee! Our mocha features a shot of bold espresso mixed with steamed milk and velvety chocolate syrup, topped with whipped cream for a decadent treat.

Macchiato

A delightful mix of bold espresso with a hint of creamy steamed milk. Whether you prefer it classic or caramel-drizzled, this coffee packs a punch while keeping it smooth.

Flat White ☕

A coffee lover’s dream! This rich and creamy drink combines smooth microfoam milk with strong espresso, delivering a velvety texture with a bold coffee taste in every sip.

Cold Brew

Slow-steeped to perfection! Our cold brew is brewed for 12-24 hours, resulting in a refreshingly smooth, naturally sweet, and less acidic coffee. Enjoy it over ice for the ultimate chilled coffee experience!
